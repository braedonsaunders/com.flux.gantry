Gantry Financial Suite is released under the MIT License. You can use, copy, modify, and distribute the project under the terms in the repository's `LICENSE` file.

## NetSuite Compatibility Note

SuiteCloud projects can be managed as normal file-based software projects. However, the SuiteCloud Terms of Service include restrictions around certain open source licenses.

For deployed SuiteApp code, avoid adding AGPL, GPL, LGPL, or similar copyleft dependencies unless you have reviewed the SuiteCloud terms and your own Oracle/NetSuite agreements.

## No Activation Required

The open source version of Gantry does not require a license key or product activation. After deploying the SuiteApp, open it and configure your dashboards, thresholds, and (optionally) an AI provider from Settings.

## Contributor Guidance

When contributing:

1. Keep deployed SuiteScript dependencies permissively licensed
2. Do not include customer data, account credentials, private keys, or screenshots with sensitive NetSuite information
3. Document any new external service dependency clearly
4. Keep features configurable so they work across customized NetSuite accounts
