## Quick Start Guide

You've installed Gantry and entered your license key. Now what? This guide walks you through your first 15 minutes with Gantry to help you get value immediately.

## Understanding the Interface

When you open Gantry, you'll see:

- **Navigation sidebar** on the left with all available dashboards
- **Main content area** showing the currently selected dashboard
- **Header** with your company name and quick actions
- **Settings gear** for configuration options

### Dashboard Navigation

Click any dashboard name in the sidebar to switch views. The currently active dashboard is highlighted. Each dashboard focuses on a specific area of financial analysis.

### Date Range Selector

Most dashboards include a date range selector at the top. This controls the time period being analyzed. Common options include:

- Last 30 days
- Last 90 days
- This fiscal year
- Custom date range

## Your First Dashboard: Liquidity

Start with the **Liquidity** dashboard to understand your cash position:

1. Click **Liquidity** in the sidebar
2. View your current cash balance at the top
3. See projected cash position based on AR and AP
4. Review the weekly cash flow forecast chart

**Key numbers to note:**
- **Total Cash** — Your current bank balance
- **Cash Runway** — How many weeks you can operate at current burn rate
- **Net Position** — Cash plus AR minus AP

> ✅ **Tip**: If your cash runway shows less than 4 weeks, the indicator turns red to alert you.

## Exploring Profitability

Next, check your **Profitability** dashboard:

1. Click **P&L** in the sidebar
2. View your overall health score (0-100, higher is better)
3. Review gross margin percentage
4. See top revenue movers and detected anomalies

The health score combines multiple factors: gross margin, revenue growth, expense control, and trends. A score above 70 indicates healthy financials.

## Checking Employee Utilization

For service businesses, the **Billable IQ** dashboard is essential:

1. Click **Billable IQ** in the sidebar
2. See overall utilization rate across the company
3. View department-by-department breakdown
4. Identify employees who may be under or over-utilized

> ℹ️ **Note**: This dashboard requires Time Tracking to be enabled in NetSuite.

## Using the AI Advisor

The AI Advisor lets you ask questions in plain English:

1. Click **Advisor** in the sidebar
2. Type a question like "What's our gross margin this quarter?"
3. Press Enter or click Send
4. Review the AI's response with supporting data

**Try these starter questions:**
- "What's our current cash position?"
- "Which customers have the most overdue invoices?"
- "How does this month's revenue compare to last month?"
- "What are our top expenses this quarter?"

## Customizing Your Experience

### Set Your Default Dashboard

Choose which dashboard appears when you first open Gantry:

1. Click the **Settings** gear icon
2. Go to **Dashboards** tab
3. Set your preferred default dashboard
4. Click Save

### Enable Dark Mode

For comfortable viewing in low light:

1. Open **Settings**
2. Toggle **Dark Mode** on
3. All dashboards instantly switch to dark theme

### Hide Unused Dashboards

If certain dashboards don't apply to your business:

1. Open **Settings**
2. Go to **Dashboards** tab
3. Toggle off dashboards you don't need
4. They'll be hidden from the navigation
