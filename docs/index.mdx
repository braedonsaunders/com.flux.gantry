## Welcome to Gantry

Gantry Financial Suite transforms your NetSuite data into actionable financial insights. Designed for finance leaders, CFOs, and accounting teams, Gantry provides real-time analytics, intelligent forecasting, and fraud detection through an intuitive dashboard experience.

## What Gantry Does

Gantry connects directly to your NetSuite account and analyzes your financial data across nine specialized dashboards. Each dashboard focuses on a critical aspect of your business:

- **Cash flow and liquidity** — Know exactly where you stand and where you're headed
- **Profitability analysis** — Understand margins by customer, department, and project
- **Employee utilization** — Track billable time and identify productivity opportunities
- **Fraud detection** — Catch anomalies before they become problems
- **Vendor performance** — Measure and manage your supply chain
- **Customer health** — Identify at-risk accounts and growth opportunities
- **Spending patterns** — Detect subscription creep and unusual spend behavior

## Key Features

### Real-Time Analytics
All dashboards pull live data from NetSuite. No waiting for batch reports or stale data. See your current position at any time.

### AI-Powered Advisor
Ask questions in plain English and get intelligent answers about your financials. "What's our cash runway?" or "Which customers are at risk of churning?" — the AI Advisor understands your business context.

### Intelligent Alerts
Gantry automatically surfaces anomalies, risks, and opportunities. Color-coded indicators help you focus on what matters most.

### Configurable Thresholds
Every organization is different. Set your own targets for gross margin, utilization rates, payment timing, and more.

### Dark Mode
Work comfortably day or night with full dark mode support across all dashboards.

## Requirements

- Active NetSuite account
- Administrator role for installation
- Standard NetSuite features (no add-ons required for basic use)

> ℹ️ **Note**: Some dashboards require specific NetSuite features. For example, the Billable IQ dashboard requires Time Tracking to be enabled. See individual module documentation for specific requirements.
